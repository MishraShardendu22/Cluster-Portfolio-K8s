## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take all security bugs in this project seriously.
Thank you for improving the security of our project. We appreciate your efforts and responsible disclosure and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a security vulnerability, please email [INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS] with a detailed description of the vulnerability and steps to reproduce it.

You can also report a security vulnerability through GitHub's private vulnerability reporting feature.

We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours, and will send a more detailed response within 48 hours indicating the next steps in handling your report. After the initial reply to your report, the security team will endeavor to keep you informed of the progress towards a fix and full announcement, and may ask for additional information or guidance.
